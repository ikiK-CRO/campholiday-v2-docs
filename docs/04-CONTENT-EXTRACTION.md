## 3. Camping Page

### Overview

> The camp has three zones for tents with electricity, parking for cars, motorcycles and also 37 pitches for car campers with electricity and water. The plots are divided into upper along the road, which passes through the camp and lower by the sea. The whole camp is located on the coast and offers a breathtaking view of the island of Brac and the mainland.

### Zone Descriptions

#### Tent Zone A
> On the coast, next to the sea in a pine forest, which provides natural shade.

**Features:**
- Parcels: A1 - A12
- Location: Seafront
- Shade: Natural pine trees
- Proximity: Direct beach access

#### Tent Zone B
> In the central part of the camp among the tall pines, decorated with Hvar stone.

**Features:**
- Parcels: B1 - B43
- Location: Central/Elevated
- Character: Traditional Hvar stone terraces
- Shade: Tall pine trees

#### Tent Zone C
> Located above the road that runs through the camp, covered with pines and contains two buildings with toilets, space for washing dishes and outdoor showers.

**Features:**
- Parcels: C1 - C30
- Location: Upper camp area
- Facilities: Toilets, dishwashing area, outdoor showers
- Shade: Pine coverage

### Booking Note
> Your head is on a pillow & your toes touch the sea.

### Minimum Stay
> The minimum stay in tents is 3 nights

## 4. Glamping Page

### Header
- **Title**: GLAMPING HVAR - ...create memories

### Main Description

> **Glamping** is where stunning nature meets modern luxury. Experiential travel is an authentic way to connect with nature. Together, the experience is about stepping off the beaten path, walking away from superficial tourist activities, and embracing an immersive cultural environment.

### Location & Setting

> **Glamping Holiday** sits on beautifully crafted Hvar stone with the trim made of traditional Dalmatian shelter. It offers seven separate tents with carefully selected names after the surrounding villages of **Gdinj, Pitve, Vrisnik, Jelsa, Ivan Dolac, Poljica, Zavala.**

### Tent Description

> Located in the pine forests with the open view of the sea and the sandy bay of Mina, the tent provides guests with the ultimate luxury glamping experience. The tent sits on a wooden deck and comes with one open-concept space, accommodating 2-3 glampers comfortably. In the open space, guests will find a large double bed, two bedside tables, a wardrobe, a sofa bed, and a safe. These tents offer modern conveniences that will make any glamper feel right at home.

### Terrace & Kitchen

> Each of the luxurious tents has a terrace with a handcrafted table and chairs to kick your feet up and enjoy the view. For your convenience, the terrace is equipped with a small kitchen consisting of two induction hobs, a refrigerator, and all necessary utensils.

### Facilities

> There are plentiful fantastic facilities on-site. All of the guests from the glamping experience use a common bathroom, which is exclusive for glamping guests. The newly built modern bathrooms feature showers, toilets, sinks, washing machines, and dryers.
